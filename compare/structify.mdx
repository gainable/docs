## Quick verdict

Dashboards tell you what happened. Apps let you do something about it. Structify focuses on collecting and structuring data into dashboards and visualizations. Gainable does the same data unification, then goes further, building full production-ready apps with forms, workflows, kanban boards, collaboration, and AI assistants on top of that unified data.

If you only need to visualize structured data, Structify does that well. If you need to act on your data, manage workflows, collaborate with your team, and build real tools, Gainable bridges the gap from data to action.

<Tip>
**Bottom line:** Structify excels at dashboards. Gainable goes from data to full working apps.
</Tip>

## Key differences that matter

### From data to action

Structify collects data and displays it in dashboards. To take action on that data, you'd use separate tools.

Gainable builds full working applications on unified data. See a deal that needs attention? Update it directly. Need to assign a task? Do it in the app. Want to discuss a data point with your team? Use the built-in chat. The gap between insight and action disappears.

### Same data intelligence, plus everything else

Gainable's [intelligent data agent](/building/data-connectors) normalizes multi-source data just like Structify, connecting to CRMs, spreadsheets, payment systems, and other sources, then unifying that data into a clean, consumable model.

The difference is what happens next. Gainable builds the full application stack on top of that unified data: [views and dashboards](/building/views), kanban boards, forms, detail pages, [team collaboration](/building/collaboration), and [Gaia Copilot](/copilots/overview) for conversational AI.

### Full application stack

Structify gives you charts and data views. Gainable gives you:

- **Charts and dashboards**: [Chart.js visualizations](/skills/chartjs) on your unified data
- **Kanban boards**: [Drag-and-drop workflow management](/skills/kanban)
- **Forms and detail views**: Full CRUD operations on your data
- **File sharing**: Upload and manage documents in context
- **Team chat**: Real-time [collaboration](/building/collaboration) right in the app
- **Gaia Copilot**: A [conversational assistant](/copilots/overview) that reads live unified data and references uploaded knowledge
- **Composable chat UI**: The [Copilot is embedded](/copilots/embedding) directly in your apps via Weavy
- **Gaia Autopilot**: [Autonomous agents](/autopilot/overview) that watch data, draft work, and land it in your inbox for approval

### Conversational and autonomous AI on your unified data

Structify focuses on structuring data. Asking intelligent questions or acting on the data requires adding separate tools.

Every Gainable app ships with [Gaia Copilot](/copilots/overview) for conversational AI and [Gaia Autopilot](/autopilot/overview) for autonomous work. Your team can ask the Copilot "which accounts haven't been contacted in 30 days?" or "summarize this quarter's revenue trend." Agents go further: they watch data against objectives and land drafts in [Autopilot](/autopilot/inbox) before anyone asks. The chat UI is powered by Weavy, embedded right in the app.

This is what bridges data to action: unified data + full apps + Gaia for both conversation and autonomy.

### Composable and extensible

Structify's primary output is dashboards. Adding new capabilities means configuring additional dashboard views.

With Gainable, your app grows with your needs. Start with a dashboard, then add a kanban board for task management. Add team chat for collaboration. Add a [Gaia Copilot](/copilots/overview) that answers questions about the data in real time, or a [Gaia Autopilot](/autopilot/overview) that watches your pipeline and drafts follow-ups. Every iteration is a natural-language prompt away. Your unified data powers all of it.

<Info>
Gainable's composable architecture means your app evolves as your needs evolve. Every new feature builds on the same intelligently unified data.
</Info>

## Pricing

Structify's pricing is based on data processing usage. Costs vary depending on the volume and complexity of data you're structuring.

Gainable charges a flat rate per builder with unlimited end users and unlimited app builds. Your data unification, app generation, and team access are all included.
